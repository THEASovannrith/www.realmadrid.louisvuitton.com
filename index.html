PARTNERSHIP

Victory Travels in Louis Vuitton

NEW ROYALTY

Louis Vuitton proudly announces its official partnership with Real Madrid,
marking a bold new chapter for the club.

Bound by a shared pursuit of excellence and the spirit of travel, Louis Vuitton
and Real Madrid unite through their rich heritages- and a mutual ambition to
inspire the new generation toward a future defined by extraordinary
achievements.

To celebrate this new partnership, the House unveils formal wear and travel
formal wear pieces for the Men's and Women's football teams along with the
Men's basketball team.

Set against the iconic backdrop of Madrid's Santiago Bernabeu Stadium, the
campaign showcases the House's tailoring expertise through an on-the-go
formal wardrobe-elevated by refined materials and custom touches that
transcend the athlete's style beyond the sport.
